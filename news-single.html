EcoFlow Team Visits Double Bliss for Sustainability Workshop

October 13, 2023 Academics

Sustainability is no longer just a word; it is a way of life that we are instilling in our students from day one.

Double Bliss Secondary School was honored to host the expert team from EcoFlow for a comprehensive workshop on renewable energy. The event focused on how modern portable power solutions and solar technology can be integrated into everyday school life.

"The brilliance shown by the students in grasping complex electrical concepts was truly inspiring." — EcoFlow Lead Engineer

Students participated in hands-on demonstrations, learning how to set up solar panels and manage energy consumption effectively. This visit marks the beginning of our "Green Campus" initiative aimed at reducing our carbon footprint.
