Double Bliss Triumphs at the Annual Inter-School Quiz Competition

November 26, 2023 Academics

Our students have once again proven that academic excellence is woven into the very fabric of Double Bliss Secondary School.

The highly anticipated Annual Inter-School Quiz Competition saw schools from across the region battle it out in subjects ranging from Mathematics and Sciences to General Knowledge and Current Affairs. Our representatives displayed exceptional speed and accuracy, securing the first-place trophy.

"It wasn't just about winning; it was about the hours of preparation and the teamwork our students displayed on stage." — HOD Academics

We extend our heartfelt congratulations to the participants and the teachers who coached them. This victory reinforces our commitment to nurturing intellectual giants who can compete on any stage.
