Welcome to Double Bliss Secondary School

At Double Bliss, we provide a dynamic and supportive environment where students thrive academically, socially, and morally. Our mission is to cultivate well-rounded individuals prepared for future challenges.

Our modern facilities and dedicated staff are committed to offering a holistic education that fosters intellectual growth, character development, and lifelong learning. Join us and become part of a community that values excellence, integrity, and respect.

Explore Our Programs

Our Mission

To provide qualitative education built on piety, discipline and hard work in grooming visionary leaders.

Our Vision

To provide a balance qualitative education, committed to the development of a total child, based on sound faith, integrity, high moral standard and versatility to compete better among peers.
