About Our School

Building a foundation for academic excellence and moral integrity.

Our History

A Tradition of Excellence Since Inception

Double Bliss Secondary School was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between high-level academic instruction and deep-rooted character development.

Over the years, we have grown from a small community initiative into a premier secondary institution, known for our modern facilities and the outstanding performance of our students in both national and international examinations.

Our Mission

To provide a world-class educational experience that empowers students with knowledge and skills to lead in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a premier center of academic excellence, recognized for developing innovative thinkers and compassionate leaders.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Discipline, and Innovation are the pillars upon which every Double Bliss student stands.

Message from the Proprietor

"Welcome to Double Bliss. Our goal is to ensure every child who passes through these gates leaves not just with a certificate, but with the confidence to change the world. We partner with parents to nurture the unique gifts of every student."

- Dr. Alhaji Abdulhakeem Ademola Adedokun, (M.Sc PhD) a.k.a IMAM
