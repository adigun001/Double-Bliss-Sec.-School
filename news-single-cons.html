Nurturing the Mind: Our Termly Student Counseling Session

April 12, 2026 Student Welfare

At Double Bliss, we believe that a healthy mind is the foundation for a successful academic career.

This term’s counseling session focused on "Emotional Intelligence and Career Pathing." Expert counselors were invited to speak with students across various levels, providing them with the tools to manage academic pressure and make informed decisions about their future professions.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world, but mental clarity is the fuel for that weapon."

The session included private one-on-one breakout groups where students felt safe to share their challenges and receive professional guidance. We continue to prioritize the holistic development of every child in our care.
